Frequently Asked Questions

Do you have any questions? See our most frequently asked questions below.

❓ What is a funnel builder?

A funnel builder helps you create a sales funnel, which is the step-by-step path visitors follow to become customers. With a WordPress funnel builder, you can build these funnels on your site using WooCommerce for payments.

👥 Who needs to use this?

Everyone who wants to convert visitors into leads or customers needs a funnel builder. It's the easiest way to funnel visitors into paying customers or more profitable ones.

🚀 What do I need to get started?

You will need a hosting account with WordPress and WooCommerce installed. The plugin integrates seamlessly with your existing WordPress setup.

💳 Which payment gateways are supported?

Any payment gateway for WooCommerce will work. We currently support Auth.Net, Mollie, PayPal, Square, Stripe, WooCommerce Payments, and Cash on Delivery for one click upsells.
